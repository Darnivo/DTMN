freq itemsets > collection of items / data that pops up often
e.g {item1, i2,i3} > itemset

support = how often an itemset shows up in a dataset

association rule > rule which indicates a certain relationship between item 
e,g {item 1, item 2} > {item 3}

confidence = how often an assoc. rule is true


apriori > algorithm to find frequent itemsets

abbreviations:
D > initial dataset
Cm > Candidate itemset N
Ln > freq. itemset N
K > the amount of individual items in a given dataset
